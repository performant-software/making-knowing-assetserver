Herbs difficult to burn in the mold

Mold them in two or three castings; being annealed, your mold will open, and it will be easier to extract the charcoal from inside.

molded letter paper

Write with some ink well thickened with gum or any other color dye which has body, and which is not erased if dampened with brandy. Then put your paper on the sheet of clay, and dampen it with brandy. Cast both sides of paper.

Adorning beds, mirrors and similar things

Model any drawing in half relief on a flat slate in order to set it on round or flat things. Cast with very fine tin, then you can gild it with gold leaf, and set it wherever you want. Fill the bottom of the relief with small rubies, orpiment, or any colored little grains.

Training a dog

You need to keep your dog attached; when it does what you command, to win its love, give it a piece of cheese that was held under the armpit. This cheese would be a bad drug if the master, or the apprentice, is red-haired.

Molded wax

Wax representations made from white lead and cerussite are not made to be burned and melted in a mold where you want to cast silver and gold; this would make it brittle. Moreover if you use such a mold, the wax would be stripped thanks to the violence of the fire which makes it boil, and the mixture made of cerrusite or any other metal would attack the mold. White wax is more delicate than the other, and it doesn't leave any filth when you want to mold it as a core, or to mold hollow.
