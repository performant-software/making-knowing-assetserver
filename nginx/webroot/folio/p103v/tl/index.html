Enema

In order for the pipe to not hurt children at all, or those with swollen or protruding hemorrhoidal veins, one covers the pipe with a parcel of chicken entrail & fold it over the end, & in this way, one gives the enema.

Cast

Tin retracts once thick, thusly it is best to cast it thin & to make two pieces out of one, then solder them if needs be. One uses a strong piece of taffeta to sieve the fine sand, with which one first covers the medal, the thickness of a teston.

To make colors fluid

Mix some vinegar with a bit of bile, & put it into a glass bottle, & if you want to preserve it for a long time, add some salt & mix some of this with your colors & that will make them flow.

Earwax

If you take enough with the tip of an ear picker & you sieve it with foam of urine, which often forms in the urine of those who have a headache, all the foam will dissipate.

Enamels

To well recognize their difference and their true color by candlelight, it is necessary to put the candle behind a crystal mirror, or a glass globe or jar full of water, because this light is like sunlight.
